About Us

Welcome to Royal PG and Hostels, where comfort meets convenience. We offer a safe, clean, and affordable living experience for students, working professionals, and anyone looking for a cozy home away from home. Our accommodations are designed with your needs in mind, providing a balanced mix of privacy and community in a welcoming environment.

At Royal PG and Hostels, we understand the importance of a peaceful living space that allows you to focus on your studies or work without distractions. With spacious rooms, modern amenities, and a range of services, we ensure that our residents have everything they need to live comfortably.

Prime Locations

Situated in key areas close to educational institutes, our properties ensure easy access to everything you need.

Safety & Security

Your safety is our priority. Our hostels are equipped with security personnel, 24x7 CCTV surveillance, and male/female wardens.

All-Inclusive Facilities

Enjoy high-speed Wi-Fi, laundry services, and fully air-conditioned rooms with balcony.

Food & Hygiene

Our spaces are designed for comfort and cleanliness, with house-keeping services and 3 meals a day.
